Our Vision

EDUBA takes its name from the ancient Babylonian schools where writing was born—spaces that transformed how people learned, communicated, and built their futures. We carry that pioneering spirit forward by focusing on education first, harnessing AI not to replace teachers, but to empower them.

We’re redefining how classrooms evolve with technology, ensuring educators have the support, training, and tools they need to ignite student curiosity and prepare the next generation for an ever-changing world.

Follow us to see how we’re shaping a future where teaching, technology, and human potential intersect in the best possible ways.

Our Team
