The First Teachers

In ancient Sumer, teachers gathered in places called Eduba's - houses of tablets where they taught students how to press meaning into clay. These weren't just schools. They were spaces where teachers shaped how new tools would change learning forever.

We took this name because we believe something simple but powerful: teachers should shape the tools they use. Not through surveys or feedback forms, but through real power to build and change educational technology.

What We're Building

    We're starting with AI because it's changing education faster than any tool since writing itself. Like those first teachers in their houses of tablets, you understand something deeper about learning than any algorithm or app.

  • A place where teachers truly lead
  • Where your classroom wisdom guides how AI and other tools work
  • Where your voice directs what we build and how we build it
  • A new way of building educational technology - one where teachers stand at the helm
