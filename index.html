Our Mission

Our mission is more than just tech; it’s about fostering a deeper understanding of the history, philosophy, and innovation within education and finding where they converge to shape the future of education.

Through interactive labs and collaborative sessions, we invite educators to engage in shaping educational technology, exploring their own ideas and concepts alongside us.

Together, we aim to:

  • Develop Educational Technology that enhances—never replaces—the role of teachers.
  • Foster creativity, collaboration, and critical thinking as the foundation of modern education
  • Ensure seamless integration of technology for real-world classrooms.

We believe in a future where educators lead the way, using technology to uplift both their teaching and their students, opening new frontiers of learning and discovery.

Who We Are

At EDUBA, we’re more than a team—we’re collaborators, innovators, and lifelong learners dedicated to empowering educators. Drawing on diverse experiences—from cryptography to marketing, from philosophy to engineering—we partner with teachers to shape tools that enhance their work, never replace it.

Inspired by the ancient Babylonian “EDUBA” schools—centers of collaboration and exploration—we believe technology is at its best when shaped by those who use it.
