Our Mission

Inspired by the ancient Babylonian “EDUBA” schools—historical centers of collaboration and learning—we embrace timeless wisdom and modern technology to equip educators with powerful, intuitive tools.

  • Develop Educational Technology that enhances—never replaces—the role of teachers.
  • Foster creativity, collaboration, and critical thinking.
  • Ensure seamless integration of technology for real-world classrooms.

We believe in a future where technology uplifts educators and students alike, opening new frontiers of learning.

About EDUBA

Inspired by the historic “Edubas” of ancient Babylon—where writing itself was born—we focus on creating modern, educator-first solutions. Our mission? Provide a collaborative space for teachers to shape technology, not be shaped by it.

Labs & Workshops

We host hands-on sessions and “Group Therapy” discussions where educators test new ideas, share challenges, and co-create solutions. These labs aren’t just demos—they’re a place to refine real-world teaching tools and inspire the next wave of innovation.
