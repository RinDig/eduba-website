About Eduba

Eduba draws inspiration from the ancient Babylonian schools—where writing was first fostered and transformed how people learned, communicated, and built their futures. Today, we carry that same spirit into modern classrooms, with a focus on empowering teachers through responsible, innovative technology.

From developing user-friendly software that streamlines daily tasks to running hands-on “Eduba Labs” that illustrate exactly when (and how) modern tools add value, we’re creating a flexible environment for educators to explore, experiment, and actively shape the next wave of teaching solutions.

Our vision is straightforward: give educators the support, training, and real-world tools they need to spark student curiosity and navigate a rapidly changing world. By continuously refining our approach in close collaboration with schools, we believe we can build a broader societal impact— because when education is at the center, everyone grows and thrives together.

Follow us as we champion a future where teaching, technology, and genuine human connection intersect in the best ways possible.

Eduba Labs & Workshops

Our “Eduba Labs” represent more than just training sessions—they’re collaborative spaces where educators can voice their day-to-day challenges, share new ideas, and explore emerging technologies firsthand. It’s not about adopting tools for the sake of it, but about shaping them to fit genuine classroom needs.

  • Group Sessions: A supportive “therapy” environment where teachers discuss current pain points with tech, compare approaches, and see live demos of evolving solutions.
  • Cohort Collaboration: We collect feedback and identify trends or unique ideas, refining our software in real-time to match what educators truly want.
  • Consultancy Mindset: Each educator becomes a “product development consultant,” guiding us to design features and interfaces that genuinely enhance teaching and learning.

By placing educators at the center, Eduba Labs become a testing ground for new approaches—allowing everyone to learn, adapt, and co-create the future of education.

Explore Our Tools

At Eduba, we’re developing two complementary tools—two sides of the same coin—designed to empower teachers and school communities to shape technology on their own terms.

Generative UI Prototype

A sandbox environment that lets educators experiment with cutting-edge interface concepts. Here, you can tinker with potential features, customize layouts, and preview how future classrooms might integrate technology. It’s a place to dream, design, and influence how next-generation tools should serve real educational needs.

Collaborative Chat & Testing

A multi-user chat platform that seamlessly connects you to different model providers. Perfect for real-time testing with colleagues, you can compare responses side-by-side, or explore collaborative workflows. Think of it as a shared space to experiment, evaluate, and refine how tech can support your teaching style.

Try the Chat Interface

Both prototypes are living projects—shaped by educator feedback and evolving classroom realities. Ultimately, our goal is to merge insights from these explorations into one (or multiple) real-world solutions that educators can fully own and adapt to their unique contexts.
